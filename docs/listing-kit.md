should link to **https://paramarines.net** (the apex — never the dev URL).
Consistent name/description across listings helps search engines connect
the entity. Update member counts occasionally; don't let listings rot.

## Long description (forums, Arma3 Units, listicle pitches)

> The Paramarine Task Force is one of Arma 3's longest-running milsim
> units — founded in 2016 and celebrating ten years of operations. We play
> a "what-if": the WW2 Paramarines carried into a modern setting as
> rapid-deployment Marines, backed by our own air wing (a fictionalized
> Marine Aircraft Group 36) flying rotary and fixed-wing support.
>
> What we offer: weekly operations (FTX Saturdays, Main Op Sundays, 8PM
> Eastern), a 17-course school pipeline from recruit training to JTAC
> certification, real rank progression tracked in our personnel system,
> a curated one-click modpack, and a 500+ member community that games
> together well beyond Arma.
>
> No milsim experience needed — every Marine starts as a Rifleman and we
> train from zero. Learn more and enlist at https://paramarines.net
